Academic Background

With extensive academic training in marine sciences, I bring a unique combination of theoretical knowledge and practical expertise to aquatic ecology education and services.

Qualifications:

  • Master's in Marine Biology with specializations in:
    • Animal Behavior
    • Biodiversity Conservation
  • Master's in Natural Resource Management

I work for an enterprise specializing in environmental engineering and aquatic ecology, bringing professional expertise to every project.

Additionally, I teach a course on the link between humans and the water world for the University of Neuchâtel. This comprehensive program explores the complex relationships between human activities and aquatic ecosystems.
