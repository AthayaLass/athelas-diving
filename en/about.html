Who I Am

About Me

Welcome to Athelas Diving. I am a passionate diving instructor, marine biologist, and environmental professional dedicated to sharing the magic of the underwater world.

My Journey

My path combines academic excellence with practical diving expertise. With two master's degrees—one in Marine Biology (specializing in animal behavior and biodiversity conservation) and another in Natural Resource Management—I bring a unique perspective to diving education and aquatic ecology.

Professional Experience

As a certified SDI instructor, I offer comprehensive diving courses and guided excursions. I work for an enterprise specializing in environmental engineering and aquatic ecology, and I teach a course on the relationship between humans and the water world at the University of Neuchâtel.

My Mission

Through Athelas Diving, I aim to help others discover the healing power of the underwater world, whether through professional diving education, ecological understanding, or mindful connection with nature. Every dive is an opportunity to experience the extraordinary beauty and wonder of marine ecosystems.
