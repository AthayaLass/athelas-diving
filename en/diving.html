SDI Instructor Qualifications

I am Antoine Baud, a certified SDI (Scuba Diving International) instructor, offering comprehensive diving education and guided excursions. My expertise includes the following specialties:

  • Night Diving - Explore the underwater world after dark and discover nocturnal marine life
  • Deep Diving - Advanced training for dives beyond 18 meters
  • Peak Performance Buoyancy - Master perfect buoyancy control and underwater movement
  • Navigation - Learn underwater navigation techniques using compass and natural references
  • Aquatic Ecology - Understand marine ecosystems, species identification, and conservation
  • Nitrox (Enriched Air) < 40% - Extend your bottom time with enriched air nitrox diving

International Certification Standards

SDI is a member of the WRSTC (World Recreational Scuba Training Council), which establishes global standards for recreational scuba diving training. These are the same international standards recognized by major diving organizations worldwide, including PADI, SSI, and others. This means your SDI certification is recognized internationally and follows the same rigorous safety and training standards as other leading certification agencies.

All certifications issued through SDI meet WRSTC standards, ensuring that your diving credentials are internationally recognized and accepted at dive centers around the world. Whether you're diving in the Caribbean, the Red Sea, or the Pacific, your SDI certification will be recognized wherever you go.
