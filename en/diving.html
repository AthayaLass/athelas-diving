SDI Instructor Qualifications

I am a certified SDI (Scuba Diving International) instructor, offering comprehensive diving education and guided excursions. My expertise includes the following specialties:

  • Night Diving - Explore the underwater world after dark and discover nocturnal marine life
  • Deep Diving - Advanced training for dives beyond 18 meters
  • Peak Performance Buoyancy - Master perfect buoyancy control and underwater movement
  • Navigation - Learn underwater navigation techniques using compass and natural references
  • Aquatic Ecology - Understand marine ecosystems, species identification, and conservation
  • Nitrox (Enriched Air) < 40% - Extend your bottom time with enriched air nitrox diving
